The Dragon
The Dragon is an old, arrogant race. Dragon warriors are strong in magic that increases strength and defense.
The Elemental
The Elemental was created by ancient dragon sorcerers. By using natural elements such as ice, fire and wind, elemental warriors are the masters of battle control, AoE damage and DoT effect.
The Demon
The Demon is who has the passion for dark power. It allows them to lower the target's defense and deal damage.
The Mech
The Mech was created by the most ancient race to serve the war. Mech warriors use spare parts and energy of dead Mech to strengthen themselves
The Beast
The Beast, which was one of the most powerful races, is now only a minority group. They are strong teammates. They often complete quests together.
The Neutral
Not being counted as a race, Neutral includes members from other races. They have their own distinct and unique characteristics. The Neutral don't follow any certain sect.
